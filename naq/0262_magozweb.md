# Magozweb

##
Kaise ǀhabe tsî ǁaxa danaǃās Nairobis ǃnâ gu ge ǁgâu-o axaga, ǃkhō-unuxa omna xu, ǃnūse gere hâ. Mâ tsēs hoasa gu ge hās ra khami gere ǃkhōǃoa. ǀGui ǁgoaga gu ge nē axaga ǁîgu di ǂgoade gere ǁguibē, ǃkhai ǀnūǃgûǃgani ai gu ge ǁomǂkhais khaoǃgâ. ǃKhaiǂkhai gu ge xui-ao gu ge ǀawon ǀkha ǀaesa gere khau. ǁÎgu ǁaegub ge Magozweb ti ǀon hâba ge hâ i. ǁÎb ge ǁîgu hoagu ǃnâ ge ǂkham i.

##
ǁÎb îra xab ge ǁōxūhe ǁaeb ǃnâb ge Magozweba ge koro kurixa i. ǁÎb omeb tawab ge ge si hâ. Nē aob ge axaba ǃgâise ge ǃkhō-unu tama hâ i. Magozwebab ge ǂauna ǂû-e ge ǂûmā tama hâ i. ǁÎb ge axaba kaise ǃgom sîsenna gere dī kai.

##
Magozweb ga nēs ǂama ǃgaesen tsî dîna dîbi, ob ge omeba gere ǂnaubi. Magozweb ge skoli ǁgab ra ǃgû ǂgao ǃkhaisa mîba bi, ob ge ǁîb omeba ǂnaubi tsî ge mî, "Kaisets gâre xū-e ǁkhāǁkhāsens ǃaroma," timi. Omeb gere ûi-ūbi ǀgaus xab ge Magozweba, ǃnona kurigu khaoǃgâ omeb omsa xu ge ǃkhoebē. ǁÎb ge ge ǃganǀgôa kai.

##
ǃGanǀgôasis ge kaise ge ǃgom i, xui-ao gu ge ǃnāsa axaga tsēs hoasa ǂû-e hōs ǀkha gere ǃgûma tsâ. ǀNî ǃnāde gu ge axaga ǀawaǃnamgu xa ǃkhōhe, tsî ǁkhāti gere ǂnauhe. ǀAesen gu ga, o gu ge ǀgui khoe-i xas tsîna ge huihe tama hâ i. ǁÎgu ge ǀkhowemâ tsî plastikna gu gere ǁamaxū tsî hōǂgā marisa xu ǀgui gere ûi. Ûib âgu ge nau mâǃoara ǃganǀgôagu di ǃnandi xa gere ǃgomǃgomhe. Nē axagu ǁîga gere ǃkhamū tsî danaǃās di hoa ǃâgu di ǀhonkhoe kai ǂgao xui-ao.

##
ǀGuitsēb ra ǀapon xawagu ǃnâ ǁhoaramâ, hîab ge Magozweba ǀoro ǁgaeǂhôa khomaiǂkhani-e ge hō. ǂKhanisa ǃanube ǀkha tsîb ge ǁgarub ǃnâ ge ǂgā. Mâ tsēs hoasab ge ǂkhanisa ūǂui tsî ai-īsiroga gere kō. Mîdeb nî khomai ǀgausab ge ge ǀū i.

##
Nē ai-īsirogu ge anikuniǁkhana-ao kai ge axab ǁgaeǂhôasa ra ǁgau. Magozweb ge anikuniǁkhana-aosis xa gere ǁhapo. ǀNî tsēdeb ge ǁîba gere mûnanai ǁîb a nē ǁgaeǂhôas ǃnâ hâ axa ti.

##
Magozweb ge ǃkhaib ǃnâ ǃgani ǃnâ mâ, tsî gere ǀkhowe. Ao-i ge ǁîb tawa ge ǃgûsi." ǃGâitsēs, tita ge Thomab ti ǀon hâ. Nēba ta ge ǀgūse ra sîsen, ǂûǁamaxū ǁgâus tawa," tib ge nē aoba ge mî. ǁÎb ge ǃhuni oms, ǀawa ǂamǃnâba ūhâs ǁga ra ǁnaellgau. "ǂGomǃgâ ta ge ra, ǁnāpa sī, tsîts ǂûxū-e nî si hōbasensa" tib ge ge mî. Aoba kō, omsa kō, tsîb ge Magozweba "ǀNîsi," ti ǃeream bi tsî ge ǃgûbē.

##
ǁKhâgu ǃkharu hâse gu ge ǃganǀgôaga ge ǁnaeti, Thomaba ǁnāpa mûmâsa. ǁÎb ge khoen ǀkha ǁgams xa ge ǃgâibahe i, ǃgōsase ǃganǀgôan ǀkha. Thomab ge khoen di ûibǁgaeǂhôana gere ǃgâ. ǁÎb ge ge taniǀnamxa-i, tsî tātsē ǂū-o tama ge ī i. ǀNî axagu ge nē ǃhuni tsî ǂhoa oms ǁga tsēs hoasa ge ǃgû tsoatsoa, tsēǃgâǁaeb ǂû-e hōs ǃaroma.

##
Magozweb ge ǀnūǃgûǃgani ai ǂnû, tsî ǂkhanis âb ǃnâ ai-īsiga gere kō, hîab ge Thomaba ǁîb xōǀkhā ge si ǂnû. "Tare-i xa ra ǃgû ǁgaeǂhôasa?" tib ge Thomaba ra dîbi. "Axab hîa ge anikuniǁkhana-ao kaib xa ra ǃgû," tib ge Magozweba ra ǃereambi. "Mâti ǀon hâ axaba?" tib ge Thomaba ge dîbi. "ǀŪ ta ge a, khomai ǁoa ta a," tib ge Magozweba tsaurase ge mî.

##
ǁKhawa kha ge ǀhao, ob ge Magozweba Thomaba ǁîb ûiǂgaroba ge ǁgamba. Nē ǂhôas ge ǁîb omeb, tsî mâti i ge hā, ob ge ǃkhoebē ǃaromas disa. Thomab ge xū-e ǃhoaǀaro tama i, tsî ǁkhāti Magozweba tare-eb nî dīsa mîba tama hâ, xaweb ge kaise ǂorisase gere ǃgâbi. ǀNî ǃnāde kha ge ǂhoa ǂamǃnâba ūhâ oms tawa gere ǁgam, ǂûǁae khara hîa.

##
Magozweb ge disi kurixa, ob ge Thomaba ǀasa ǁgaeǂhôa ǂkhanisa ge ǀkhaebi. Nē ǂkhanis ge ǃgaroǃā-axab hîa ǂanǂansa ǂaiǃgaiǀhuru-aose ge ǂansa ib xa gere ǃgû. ǂGui ǃnādeb ge Thomaba nē ǁgaeǂhôasa Magozweba gere khomaiba, ǀguitsēb ge mîs kōse. "ǂÂi ta ge ra tita nî skolǃgû tsoatsoa tsî nî khomai ǃkhaisa. Mâtits ra ǂâi?" Thomab ge ra mîba bi, ǁîb ǀgôan ra hâ tsî skolǃgû ǃkhai-eb ǂan ti.

##
Magozweb ge nē ǀasa hâǃkhaib, tsîb nî ǁîba skolǃgû se i ǃkhais xa gere ǂâi. ǁÎb omeb ge mî-e ǀnîsi ama tama hâ, tsîb ǁîba ǁkhāǁkhāsens ǃaroma ǀnîsi kaise gâre? Nē ǀasa hâǃkhaib tawa ta xare ǃkhāmûhe tide? ǁÎb ge ge ǃao, tsî "ǃGangu ǃnâ hâmas ge tsâse a ǃgâi," ti gere ǂâi.

##
Thomab ǀkhab ge ǁîb ǃaoba ge ǀgoragu. ǁAe-i khaoǃgâb ge nē aoba axaba ge mîǁawoǁawoba, ǃgâi ûibab nî nē ǀasa hâǃkhaib tawa ūhâ ǃkhaisa.

##
ǁNātimib ge Magozweba ǀgam ǀkhara axakha ǀkha ǁomǃnâǃnā-omsa ra ǀgoraguse, oms ǃnâ ge doeǂgâ. Hoan ǀhaob ai i ge disi ǀgôana nē oms ǃnâ gere hâ. ǁÎn ge maro Cissys, tsî ǁîs aob, ǃnona arigu, ǀhôas tsî kaira piris tsîn ǀkha ge ǁanǁare hâ i.

##
Magozweb ge skola ge ǂgâ tsoatsoa, xawe i ge ge ǃgom i. ǂGui xūnab ge nî sīǃnâse ge ī i. ǁÎb ge skolǂgâsa gere ǀû ǂgao. Xaweb ge ge ǂâihō anikuniǁkhana-aob tsî ǂaiǃgaiǀhuru-aob hâkha xa ra ǃgû ǁgaeǂhôa ǂkhanira xa. ǁÎkha khamib ge ge ǀûsen tama hâ i.

##
Magozweb ge ǃam ǂamǃnâba ūhâ oms ǃgâb ai, skoli di ǁgaeǂhôa ǂkhanisa gere khomai. Thomab ge ǁîb xōǀkhā ge si ǂnû. "Tare- i xa ra ǃgû ǁgaeǂhôasa?" tib ge Thomaba ge dîbi. "ǁGauǃnâ-ao kai ǂgao ra axab xa ra ǃgû," tib ge Magozweba ge ǃeream. "Mâti ǀon hâ axaba?" tib ge Thomaba ge dîbi. "ǁÎb ǀons ge a Magozwe," tib ge Magozweba ǀnom rase ge ǃereambi.
