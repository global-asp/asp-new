# ǃKhaon ǀûna ūhâba tama ǃaromas xa

##
ǀGuitsēs ge ǃÔasa ǃāb xōǀkhā ge ǃgû garu i.

##
ǃKhaos tsîn ge ge hâ i, ǁîs ge ǂause gere ǃgûmâ tsî ǁkhoaxa ǃam ǀgâna gere ǂû.

##
ǃKhaos ge ǃôas tsîn ǁnapa ra hâma ǃkhaisa ge mû tama hâ i, tsî ǃÔasa ǂais ai ge dāhō. ǃÔas ge ǃKhaos ai ge ǃau tsoatsoa, "Sa ǃKhaosa! ǂAis ais go dā-aite ǃkhaisas mû tama hâ?"

##
ǃKhaos ge ǃÔas ai ǀûbaba ge ǂgan, "Mû si tama ta ge go hâ i, xuige toxoba ǀûba te re!" Xawes ge ǃÔasa ge ǁnâuǃā ǂgao tama hâ i, tsî ǃKhaos ai ge ǃau, "Sas ge ǂâibasens ǀkha ǁnāsa go dī! ǀGuitsēs nî mû! Tsamas nî!"

##
ǃÔas ge ǀAes tawa sī, tsî ra mîba, "ǃGû i ǃKhaosa ǁgamma xus ǂoaxa tsî ǀgâna ra ǂû hîa ǂhuwi. ǁÎs go dā aite xui-ao!" ǃÔa, ǃgomsi i a ǀkhai ti ǀhōse, tis ge ǀAesa ge ǃeream si. Sās ra ǂgan khami ta ge nî dī.

##
ǃKhaos ge ega ǃāba xu ǃnuse ǀgâna gere ǂû. "ǂAnini!" Tis ge ǀAesa ge ǂhuwi tsoatsoa. ǀAeǁhawub ge ǃKhaos ǀûga ge ǂhuwiǁnâ.

##
ǃKhaos ge ā tsoatsoa tsî ǁgammi ǁga ge ǃkhoe. Hoa ǀûgu âs ge ǀaes xa ge ǂhuwiǁnâhe. ǃKhaos ge kaise gere ā, "Ti ǀûn ge ǀAes xa go ǂhuwiǁnâhe!" Hoa ǀûn ge go ǁgôa! Ae, ti îxa ǀûna!"

##
ǃÔas ge ǃKhaos ǀûn ge ǂhuwiǁnâ ǃkhais xa gere ǃgâiaǂgao. Nētsēs koses ge ǃKhaosa ǀaesas ra ǃao xui-ao, ǁgamma xu ǃnuse ǃgû tama hâ.
