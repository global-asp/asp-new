# Kenga vikoroma

##
Kenga vikorama.

##
Ngombe kukumba,"Moo."

##
Shipendje kukumba, "Meeh, meeh."

##
Kakambe kulilikida, "Riririri."

##
Shinguru kughona,"Gruuuu."

##
Litende kukekekeda, "Krikekeke."

##
Mbwa kughuda,"Huhuhu."

##
Munafarama kughamba, "Shihh!"
