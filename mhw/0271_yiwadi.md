# Yiwadi

##
Twoko tuwadi totumbiru to kukwatitha.

##
Tughuru totumbiru tuwadi tokuthititha.

##
Twiho tuwadi totumbiru to kumonitha.

##
Tutwi tuwadi totumbiru tokuyuvitha.

##
No moko mawadi ghoruhakitho ghokundhondheritha.
